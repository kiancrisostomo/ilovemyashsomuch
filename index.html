💌 A Letter For You

✨ 💫 ⭐
My Baby, 🐾

I want to start by saying sorry for how I acted earlier. I'm sorry if I made you feel like I didn't believe you or that I didn't trust you. The truth is, I do trust you. I always have.

What I struggle with sometimes isn't my trust in you — it's the doubts I have about myself. There are moments when I blame myself for the things that happened to me before, and I end up wondering if maybe I was the reason those things happened. Because of that, I become afraid. I become scared of losing the one person I trust the most.

And that person is you, baby.

I know my words and actions earlier may have hurt you, and I'm truly sorry. When I opened our conversation, that was the first thing I brought up, and I wish I had approached things differently. The truth is, I miss you so much. More than I can properly explain.

I've also been struggling with insecurities about myself — my body, my flaws, and the things I feel I'm lacking. Sometimes those insecurities become louder than they should, and they affect the way I think. But despite all of that, you've continued to believe in me. You've supported me, reassured me, and seen something good in me even when I couldn't see it myself.

Thank you for that, baby.

I promise I'll do better. I promise I'll work on believing in myself the same way you believe in me. I don't want my fears and insecurities to make you feel like I don't trust you, because you never deserved that. You deserve someone who trusts you fully, and I want to be that person.

And if you'll allow me, I want to ask for something from you.

Please let me have your heart again.

Please allow me to continue loving you the way I always have. I know I made mistakes, and I know I hurt you, but I hope you can find it in your heart to forgive me. I still want to be the one who makes you smile, the one who stays, and the one who loves you through everything.

I love you so much, baby. More than these words could ever fully express.

Always,
Your Baby ❤️ 🐾
